    Material Tip: Wooden-handled utensils must be hand washed and dried promptly.

    1. Safety: Do not leave knives submerged in water.
    2. Rinse immediately after use.
    3. Wash by hand with warm water and mild soap.
    4. Scrub from spine to edge—never toward your fingers.
    5. Dry immediately to prevent rust.
    6. Store in a knife block, magnetic strip, or sheath.

    Extra: Never put quality knives in the dishwasher. Carbon steel knives need occasional food-grade oil to prevent rust.
