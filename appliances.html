    3. Wipe interior walls and gasket using warm water + dish soap. For stains, scrub gently with a baking soda paste.

  • Safety: Ensure all burners are off and completely cool.

    1. Remove grates and burner caps; soak in hot soapy water (or vinegar + baking soda).
    2. Scrape burnt food gently; wipe surface with warm soapy water.
    3. Use baking soda paste on stubborn spots; let sit 10–15 minutes before scrubbing.
    4. Dry and replace burner parts; wipe knob area carefully (avoid excess water).

  • Safety: Oven must be off and cool. For self-cleaning mode, follow the manual.

    1. Remove racks; soak and scrub in hot soapy water.
    2. Sprinkle baking soda inside, spray with water, let sit for several hours or overnight.
    3. Scrub and wipe clean; use oven cleaner only if needed and ventilate well.
    4. Clean door glass with glass cleaner or vinegar solution.
    5. Dry and replace racks.

    Frequency: Clean spills ASAP; deep clean every 3–6 months.
