1. Cool the pan to room temperature (don’t immerse a hot pan in cold water).
    2. Dump out leftover food and scrape gently with a rubber spatula.
    3. Rinse under warm running water to remove loose bits.
    4. Add dish soap + warm water to soak if needed.
    5. Scrub with soft sponge; use baking soda for stubborn spots.
    6. Rinse thoroughly.
    7. Dry immediately to avoid water spots.

      8. **Material notes: for nonstick saucepans, never use metal scrubbers; for stainless steel, a paste of baking soda + water helps burnt-on food.**
